🥪 Sandwich Recipe

Description

A vegetable sandwich is a quick and healthy snack made with fresh vegetables, butter, and cheese between slices of bread. It is easy to prepare and perfect for breakfast, lunch, or an evening snack.

Ingredients

  • 4 bread slices
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cheese slices
  • salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Tomato ketchup (optional)

Steps

  1. Spread butter evenly on all bread slices.
  2. Arrange the tomato, cucumber, and onion slices on one slice of bread.
  3. Place the cheese slices on top of the vegetables
  4. Sprinkle salt and black pepper.
  5. Cover with another bread slice.
  6. Grill or toast the sandwich until golden brown, or serve it fresh.
  7. Cut the sandwich into halves and serve with tomato ketchup.
