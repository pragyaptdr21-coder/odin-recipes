🍝 Pasta Recipe

Description

Creamy white sauce pasta is a simple and delicious meal made with pasta, vegetables, milk, butter, and cheese. It is a comforting dish that can be prepared in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup grated cheese
  • ½ cup sweet corn
  • ½ cup capsicum
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta until tender and drain the water.
  2. Melt butter in a pan.
  3. Add flour and cook for one minute.
  4. Gradually pour in the milk while stirring.
  5. Add cheese, salt, and black pepper.
  6. Mix in the vegetables and cooked pasta.
  7. Cook for 2-3 minutes and serve hot.
