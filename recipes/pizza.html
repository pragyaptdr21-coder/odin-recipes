🍕Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a crispy baked crust topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and your favorite vegetables. It is easy to customize and perfect for sharing with family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base
  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese (grated)
  • ½ onion (sliced)
  • ½ capsicum (sliced)
  • ½ cup sweet corn
  • ½ teaspoon oregano
  • ½ teaspoon chili flakes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (430°F).
  2. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the pizza base.
  3. Add mozzarella cheese on top.
  4. Arrange the onion, capsicum, and sweet corn evenly.
  5. Sprinkle oregano and chili flakes.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the cheese melts and the crust turns golden.
  7. Slice the pizza and serve hot.
